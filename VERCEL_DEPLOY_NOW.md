## STEP BY STEP

### Step 1: Click "Sign Up"
You'll see "Sign Up" in the top right.

### Step 2: Click "Continue with GitHub"
Pick the GitHub option.

### Step 3: Click "Authorize"
GitHub will ask for permission. Click Authorize.

### Step 4: You're in Vercel!
You should see a dashboard.

### Step 5: Click "Add New..."
Look for a button that says "Add New..." at the top left.

### Step 6: Click "Project"
A menu will appear. Click "Project".

### Step 7: Find Your Repo
You'll see your GitHub repos listed.
Find `polished-prompt`
Click it.

### Step 8: Click "Import"
There's a big button that says "Import". Click it.

### Step 9: Click "Deploy"
**Wait!** Before you click Deploy, scroll down...

### Step 10: Find "Environment Variables"
Look for a section called "Environment Variables".

### Step 11: Add Your Railway URL
Click "Add" or the "+" button

Fill in:
- **Name:** `VITE_API_URL`
- **Value:** Paste your Railway URL here!

Example: `https://polished-prompt.up.railway.app`

### Step 12: NOW Click "Deploy"
The big blue "Deploy" button.

### Step 13: Wait
It will say "Building..." and show progress.
Wait 3-5 minutes.
